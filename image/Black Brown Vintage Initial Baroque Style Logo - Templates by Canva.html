Printable hotel logos by Canva

It takes more than a nice, sturdy building to make a successful hotel. You need a brand and name that people will remember and come to love. And the first step to achieving this is to design the best hotel logo.

If youre looking for inspiration, start your search among Canvas professionally made hotel logo templates. We have ready-made logo layouts that you can use for a new hotel emblem. These designs are free to use, edit, and print. You dont need a design background because the editor is beginner-friendly. With Canva, everyone can be a designer.

Start with a hotel logo template and work your magic like a pro. If you like the template as it is, just make minimal edits, like changing the colors and the font. You can also move the elements around and adjust their sizes accordingly. Do you have a photo or illustration that you want to use? Upload it to Canva and drag it to your canvas. You can also edit pictures and add photo effects. With Canvas easy-to-use platform, youll create a boutique or luxury hotel logo design in no time.

If you want to level up your design, Canva Pro is for you. It gives you access to premium templates, design elements, and fonts for your logo. Resizing is also a breeze, thanks to the premium, one-click Magic Resize tool. This is useful when printing your logo on different marketing collaterals and posting it on different social media platforms.

Before finalizing your free hotel logo, you can share the template with your team for comments and edits. Canva lets you share the design link for either viewing or editing, making collaborations easier. Once done, download the final free hotel logo design for your marketing needs. You can also share your fresh logo via email or your social media accounts.
